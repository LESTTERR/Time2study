Key Features

Class & Task Management

Easily add, update, and manage your classes and tasks.

Chatbot Assistant

Interact with your schedule using a chatbot check tasks, add class by simply chatting.

Automatic Calendar

Your calendar auto-updates daily and displays your schedule. Deadlines are marked with dots so you never miss anything.

Analytics Dashboard

Track your academic progress with real-time analytics on your total classes, tasks, pending items, and completed work.

Deadline Indicators

Red dots appear on the calendar to highlight task deadlines, giving you a quick overview of urgent items each day.

Progressive Web App

Install it like an app and enjoy a smooth, fast, mobile-friendly experience from any device.
